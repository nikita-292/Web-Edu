Facing an Issue?

Check out our frequently faced issues below. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by visiting the login page and clicking on the "Forgot Password" link.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can change your email address in your account settings.

How can I access a lecture of the course?

First, log in to your account and navigate to the dashboard. Choose or click on the course you want to access.

On the left side, you will find an index of course content. Scroll through and find the lecture you wish to watch.

Click on the lecture to view its contents.

What should I do if I am facing a lag issue in video recordings?

If you are experiencing a lag issue in video recordings, please create a screen recording of the issue you are facing and provide the details using the Support Form .
