Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Course Description

This comprehensive course provides an introduction to the field of artificial intelligence (AI), covering fundamental concepts and techniques used to create intelligent systems. Topics include machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, this course will help you understand the basics of AI and its applications.
